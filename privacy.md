# Privacy Policy for GenDash

**Last Updated: April 27, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how GenDash handles your information. 

### 1. No Data Collection
GenDash is a local-first application. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, generator telemetry, or financial information to any external servers.

### 2. Data Storage
All data entered into the App, including fuel levels, maintenance logs, and generator status, is stored locally on your device. If you choose to use the Home Assistant integration, that data is sent only to your own self-hosted Home Assistant instance via your local network.

### 3. In-App Purchases
All transactions are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your credit card information, bank details, or billing address.

### 4. Third-Party Services
The App does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. 

### 5. Your Rights
Since no data is collected by the developer, you have total control over your information. Deleting the App will permanently delete all local data associated with it.

### 6. Contact
For any questions regarding this policy, please contact support through the GenDash GitHub repository: https://github.com/marcoue/GenDash-Support/
