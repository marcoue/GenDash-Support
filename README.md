# Privacy Policy for GenDash

**Last Updated:** April 27, 2026

At GenDash, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data within the GenDash application.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
* **Location Data:** GenDash requests access to your precise location to determine altitude. This data is used solely to calculate real-time fuel consumption penalties for your generator. This data is processed locally on your device and is **not** stored, shared, or linked to your identity.
* **Local Network:** GenDash requires local network access to communicate with your Home Assistant instance. This communication happens directly between your device and your server.

## 2. Third-Party Services
GenDash connects to your self-hosted **Home Assistant** instance. We do not have access to your Home Assistant credentials or the data transmitted between the app and your instance.

## 3. Data Storage
All configuration settings (such as server URLs and generator profiles) are stored locally on your device using standard iOS encryption (UserDefaults and SwiftData). We do not use any external cloud databases to store your personal information.

## 4. Quebec Law 25 Compliance
As a developer based in Quebec, I am committed to protecting your personal information. Since no personal data is collected or stored on external servers, there is no data to disclose or delete upon request.
